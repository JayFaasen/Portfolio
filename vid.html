Boekenzoeker

The assignment

I made this in year 1, I had to create a bookfinder for young students, 12-15 years old, so they could easily find books by answering a few questions and put them on a reading list, which would be able to be mailed or printed out. This was for the libraries for Gemeente Amsterdam. I had to design it for a tablet size.

My idea

I had to create my own corporate identity and also combine the one from Gemeente Amsterdam. I choose the creative way and put in a kind of gaming theme. Since theres a lot of childeren who likes game, i thought why not try to make it kind of like a game. I made this clickable prototype in Figma
